How I fell in love with UX

I was a software developer when it all started. We were developing the coolest feature... that was not working. Why?

The Product Design team decided to conduct usability tests to figure out what was wrong. They felt I had liking for this "UX stuff" so they brought me in.

Appalled, I watched the users struggle time after time over the code I so carefully worked on.

I started to empathise with them and slowly it became my role to watch over our users.

Quickly enough, I made my way to the Product Design team and UX became a part of my life.
